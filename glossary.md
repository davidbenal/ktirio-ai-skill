# Glossary — Archviz Terminology for AI Prompts

Use these terms in your prompts for more precise AI-generated architectural renders. Organized by category.

---

## Materials — Wood

| Term | Description | Use in prompt |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **European oak** | Light to medium brown, visible grain, warm | "European oak wide-plank flooring" |
| **Walnut** | Dark brown with rich grain pattern | "American walnut veneer cabinetry" |
| **Teak** | Golden brown, weather-resistant, tropical | "teak outdoor decking" |
| **Pine** | Light, knotty, rustic | "natural pine ceiling beams" |
| **Ebony** | Very dark, near black, luxurious | "ebony-stained wood paneling" |
| **Bamboo** | Light, uniform, sustainable | "bamboo strip flooring" |
| **Reclaimed wood** | Weathered, varied tones, character | "reclaimed timber accent wall" |
| **Whitewashed** | Wood with white/gray wash, grain visible | "whitewashed oak floorboards" |
| **Charred / Shou sugi ban** | Blackened wood, Japanese technique | "shou sugi ban cedar cladding" |

## Lighting terms

| Term | Description | Use in prompt |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Golden hour** | Warm, low-angle sunlight, long shadows | "golden hour sunlight from the west" |
| **Blue hour** | Cool twilight, sky glows, interior lights on | "blue hour exterior with warm interior glow" |
| **Ambient occlusion** | Soft shadows in crevices, adds depth | "subtle ambient occlusion in corners" |
| **Volumetric light** | Visible light rays through atmosphere | "volumetric light rays through tall windows" |
| **Rim lighting** | Light outlining edges of objects | "rim lighting on furniture edges" |
| **Bounce light** | Light reflecting off surfaces, fills shadows | "warm bounce light from wooden floor" |
| **Cove lighting** | Hidden LED strip in ceiling cove | "cove lighting in suspended ceiling" |
| **Uplighting** | Light pointing upward, dramatic | "landscape uplighting on facade" |
| **Downlighting** | Recessed ceiling lights | "recessed downlights over kitchen island" |
| **Pendant** | Hanging light fixture | "cluster of glass pendant lights" |
| **Sconce** | Wall-mounted light | "minimal wall sconces in corridor" |
| **Zenithal** | Light from directly above (skylight) | "zenithal natural light from roof opening" |

## Architectural styles

| Style | Key features | Prompt keywords |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Modern** | Clean lines, flat roofs, glass, open plans | "modern architecture, clean lines, minimal ornamentation" |
| **Contemporary** | Current trends, mixed materials, organic forms | "contemporary design, mixed materials, fluid forms" |
| **Minimalist** | Reduction to essentials, monochrome, void | "minimalist interior, white walls, sparse furniture" |
| **Industrial** | Exposed structure, raw materials, loft | "industrial loft, exposed brick, steel beams, concrete floor" |
| **Scandinavian** | Light wood, white, functional, cozy (hygge) | "Scandinavian interior, light oak, white walls, warm textiles" |
| **Mediterranean** | Warm tones, terracotta, arches, courtyards | "Mediterranean villa, terracotta roof, stucco walls, courtyard" |
| **Tropical modern** | Open-air, vegetation, natural materials | "tropical modern house, open plan, lush vegetation, timber" |
| **Brutalist** | Raw concrete, geometric, monumental | "brutalist building, exposed concrete, geometric volumes" |
| **Japanese / Wabi-sabi** | Imperfection, natural, simple, timber | "Japanese-inspired interior, shoji screens, tatami, wood" |
| **Art Deco** | Geometric patterns, gold, luxury, symmetry | "art deco lobby, geometric patterns, brass details, marble" |
| **Biophilic** | Nature integration, plants, natural light, water | "biophilic office, living walls, natural light, water feature" |
| **Parametric** | Algorithm-driven forms, organic geometry | "parametric facade, flowing organic forms, perforated panels" |

## Composition terms

| Term | Meaning | Use in prompt |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Vignette** | Darker edges framing the image | "subtle vignette focusing on center" |
| **Bokeh** | Blurred background, shallow depth of field | "shallow depth of field with soft bokeh" |
| **Tilt-shift** | Miniature effect, selective focus plane | "tilt-shift aerial view" |
| **Two-point perspective** | Two vanishing points, no vertical convergence | "two-point perspective, straight verticals" |
| **One-point perspective** | Single vanishing point, symmetrical | "one-point perspective down the corridor" |
| **Hero shot** | The main, most impressive view | "hero shot of the main facade at golden hour" |
| **Vignette shot** | Detail or atmosphere focus, partial view | "vignette of kitchen counter with morning light" |
| **Context shot** | Building in its urban/landscape setting | "context shot showing building in streetscape" |

## Real estate marketing terms

Use these when generating renders for property marketing:

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Hero image** | Main marketing image, most impressive angle |
| **Lifestyle shot** | Shows people or implied living in the space |
| **Amenity render** | Pool, gym, lounge — shared facility visualization |
| **Street view** | Building as seen from public sidewalk |
| **Aerial masterplan** | Bird's eye view of entire development |
| **Unit interior** | Individual apartment/unit visualization |
| **Lobby render** | Building entrance and reception area |
| **Facade study** | Multiple material/color options for building exterior |
| **Night render** | Evening scene showing lighting design |
| **Seasonal render** | Same view in different seasons (marketing calendars) |
