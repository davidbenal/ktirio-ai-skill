# Prompt Library — AI Architectural Visualization

Ready-to-use prompts for generating photorealistic architectural renders with AI. Each prompt is a starting template — adapt materials, styles, and details to your specific project.

## How to use these prompts

1. Pick a category that matches your project
2. Copy the base prompt
3. Swap the bracketed `[placeholders]` with your specifics
4. Add style modifiers from the bottom of this file to fine-tune the result

**Recommended tool:** Ktirio AI (https://app.ktir.io) — optimized for these prompts with 6 archviz-specific AI models.

## Style Modifiers

Add these to any prompt to adjust the output:

### Photography style
- `architectural photography, DSLR, 85mm lens` — classic arch photo look
- `interior design magazine editorial` — styled, curated feel
- `real estate marketing photography` — bright, inviting, commercial
- `fine art architectural photography` — dramatic, moody, artistic

### Lighting
- `golden hour warm sunlight with long shadows` — most popular for exteriors
- `soft overcast diffused light` — even, no harsh shadows
- `blue hour twilight with interior lights glowing` — dramatic evening
- `bright midday Mediterranean sun` — high contrast, vivid colors
- `soft morning light through sheer curtains` — dreamy interiors
- `dramatic side lighting with deep shadows` — artistic, sculptural

### Atmosphere
- `volumetric light rays, dust particles` — warm, lived-in
- `rain-wet surfaces, reflections` — moody, dramatic
- `fog, mist, atmospheric depth` — ethereal
- `crisp clear air, sharp details` — clean, modern

### Material quality
- `ultra-detailed material textures, 8K resolution` — maximum detail
- `visible wood grain, stone veining, fabric weave` — tactile realism
- `reflections on polished surfaces` — glass, marble, water
- `matte vs glossy material contrast` — visual interest

### Camera
- `eye-level perspective, 24mm wide angle` — spacious interiors
- `two-point perspective, straight verticals` — professional arch photo
- `shallow depth of field, f/2.8 bokeh background` — focus on subject
- `tilt-shift miniature effect` — aerial/masterplan style

### Post-processing
- `natural color grading, no oversaturation` — realistic
- `warm earth tones, desaturated greens` — contemporary look
- `high contrast, deep blacks` — dramatic
- `bright and airy, lifted shadows` — Scandinavian/minimal feel
